A bit of history

Konkan Tools Pvt Ltd was established in 1996 by Mr.Vinayak Shanbhag with the objective of providing services to the manufacturing industry. We have grown with time and extended to product based solutions and services in the field of electronics and medicine. Konkan Tools Pvt Ltd specialises in mechanical assembly,jigs and fixture design,prototyping,injection moulding and custom-made engineering parts. As a team, we assure timely delivery and superior quality at competitive cost.
