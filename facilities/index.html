Over the years


2006
Vrunda Associates was established in Sinnar,Nashik.The assembly line was extended to the new workshop to meet the increasing demands.

2004
Konkan Techno Services was establised in Sinnar,Nashik.
It is an assembly line for Lexus type of switches with an inhouse testing and laser printing facilities.It has an installed capacity of 15 lakh switches per month.

2002
Konkan Modular Fittings was established at MIDC Sinnar,Nashik.
The assembly line for modular switches has an installed capacity of 10 lakh switches per month.

2000
Konkan Technologies was established in Sinnar,Nashik.
The moulding and press shop here has 8 automatic injection moulding machines of L&T Demag and Ferrowmatic Milicron make.The presses are of capacity 100,50 and 30 ton with shearing capacity of upto 4mm thickness.

1996
Konkan Tools Pvt Ltd was established in Ambad,Nashik.It started as a press shop with shearing capacity upto 4 mm thickness.As of today,it has a well laid floor plan with programmable and manual equipments for providing mechanical assemly,jigs and fixtures and custom components.
